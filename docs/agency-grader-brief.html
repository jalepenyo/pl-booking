Agency Grader

Free digital presence audit for insurance agencies → demo bookings for PolicyLift

March 2026 Strategy Approved Phase 1 Built

What is it?

An agency owner types their business name, and in 30 seconds gets a scored audit of their online presence. No signup, no email gate. The full report is free. The CTA is "Book a Demo."

The model: Owner.com's Restaurant Grader, HubSpot's Website Grader. Our twist — Google Places identifies the agency automatically. Every gap maps to a PolicyLift product. The conversion event is a demo booking, not an email capture.

The Funnel = The Report

Every check is something most agencies will fail. No filler.

Decisions Already Made

✓No email gate. Google Places gives us the lead. CTA is demo booking.
✓Funnel is the report. No separate category names competing with funnel stages.
✓Score out of 100. Weighted 50/25/25 across 3 scored stages.
✓"Unable to verify" ≠ failure. Honest about detection limits. Neutral icon, asterisk on score.
✓Discovery is the SEO teaser. Full SEO audit report is the upsell from the demo.
✓No filler checks. Every check must be something most agencies fail.
✓Honest scoring — no gated points. If an agency earns 100/100, they get it. No artificial ceiling that forces a sale. Most agencies will fail plenty of checks on their own.
✓CTA earns the meeting, doesn't extort it. The grader builds trust by being genuinely useful. The CTA asks the question they can't answer: "You scored 62 on the top of your funnel. But what happens after someone fills out that form at 11pm?" Two value props — fix what's broken (grader) + automate what you can't (PolicyLift).
